The Problem

Intentional or not, erroneous information can undermine social and political stability of a community

Our Goal

Warning: Our research aims to help social media channels flag messages so that users are better informed about the reliability of messages they transmit

Caution: We propose to investigate how social media technologies can assist users to better protect themselves against unintentional misinformation propagation

Resistance: We target to improve the resilience of modern social media-driven information channels by developing and evaluating detection and dousing strategies to respond to social cyber-attacks on social media channels .
