Ambiguity Parser

The goal of Ambiguity Parser is to reduce ambiguity of any sentence by generating 3 phase trees of that Sentence.

It also gives out the probability score of those 3 trees, depicting which one is the best tree.

For Example, Here's a sentence:

The man saw the girl using a telescope.

This is an ambiguous sentence. We cannot interpret this sentence correctly, because there are 2 following Contexts:

Either the man used the telescope to see the girl OR

The girl was using a telescope and the man saw her.
